Also because the name describes the plurality of a Blog site, in which many people publishes many topics, which will be equivalent to a party (a fandango) with many people talking at the same time about many things.

    Faithful to Mexican tradition, we are calling our blog posts "Pegostes", which is a kind of messing thing to do, thus authors are our "Pegosteadores".

    Hopefully, you will find this fandango of pegostes in a merequetengue of your liking.
